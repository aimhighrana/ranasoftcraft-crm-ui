Welcome to MDO

Welcome to Master Data Online! Master Data Online (MDO) delivers trustworthy and reliable data to business users by mediating, managing and governing core master data shared by multiple master data domains. It enables business users to easily create information with standardized business rules, workflows and approval processes. Available both on cloud and enterprise models MDO has the ability to integrate with SAP, Salesforce, Microsoft and other leading enterprise solutions.
