Welcome to Master Data Online

Master Data Online (MDO) delivers trustworthy and reliable data to business users. By mediating, managing and governing master data it can be shared by multiple master data domains. It enables business users to easily create information with standardized business rules, workflows and approval processes. Available both on cloud and enterprise models, MDO can integrate with SAP, Salesforce, Microsoft and other leading enterprise solutions.
